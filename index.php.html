The 6 dimensions

Attention isn't one thing.
It's six measurable signals.

The way SEO has rankings. The way GEO has citations. BAO has attention scores. Each dimension maps a specific cognitive mechanism that determines whether your content survives the brain's filtering process.

  • Hook Strength
    Measures scroll-stop power in the first 300ms. Does your opening frame trigger the brain's threat/reward scanner?
  • Curiosity Gap
    Measures the brain's reward anticipation signal. Does your content create a question the brain needs to answer?
  • Identity Resonance
    Measures niche recognition. Does the brain immediately identify this content as relevant to its current attentional template?
  • Retention Prediction
    Estimates completion probability based on dopamine reward pacing. Where does the anticipation engine shut down?
  • Cognitive Ease
    Measures mental processing cost per frame. High cognitive load without reward = swipe. This catches that risk.
  • Emotional Density
    Measures affective engagement per second. Emotion is the brain's primary retention mechanism — this maps its presence.
